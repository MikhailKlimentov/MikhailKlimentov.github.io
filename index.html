My name is Mikhail Klimentov (or @LeaderGrev on Twitter). I currently work at Atlantic 57, The Atlantic's digital consultancy, as an associate editor. I'm also the deputy editor of BRINK News, a daily publication which covers new thinking on corporate risk and resilience in the global economy. I have written and recruited pieces on a diverse range of topics for the site, including interviews with Tim Wu about antitrust enforcement and with a former WTO lawyer about the language around trade and globalization, and pieces about gender divisions and labor as well as tech and human trafficking.

I'm also an editor and contributor to Atlantic 57's flagship weekly newsletter, the Five/Seven, which covers trends, best practices, and (sometimes!) worst practices in digital media.

I love comedy, the oxford comma, and parenthetical asides (among other things). This site is super under construction, but I just love to brag about this sweet, sweet, url.
